Privacy Policy

At Bukit Pandan Hotel, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Personal Identification Information: Name
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our website and services.

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to:

  • Process your bookings and provide customer support.
  • Improve our website and services based on your feedback.
  • Send promotional emails and updates, if you have opted in.

Data Security

We implement strict security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell or share your data with third parties except as required by law or for the purposes of providing our services (such as payment processing partners).

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. For any privacy-related inquiries, please contact us at privacy@bukitpandanhotel.com.

Thank you for trusting Bukit Pandan Hotel with your data. We are committed to respecting your privacy and ensuring your information is safe.
