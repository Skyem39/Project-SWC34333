About Us

Welcome to Bukit Pandan Hotel, where luxury meets comfort. Our story began in 2020, with the goal of offering world-class hospitality and creating unforgettable experiences for our guests.

Located in the heart of Kuala Lumpur, our hotel offers a wide range of amenities, from luxurious rooms and suites to top-notch dining and recreational facilities.

We're not just a hotel; we're a home away from home. Our dedication to providing exceptional service and unforgettable experiences has earned us a place in the International Hotel Awards.
