Our Facilities

At Bukit Pandan Hotel, we offer a range of facilities designed to ensure your stay is as comfortable and enjoyable as possible.

Our infinity pool offers a breathtaking view of the city skyline, perfect for relaxing or enjoying a refreshing dip.

Fitness Center

Stay fit and healthy during your stay at our state-of-the-art fitness center, fully equipped with modern machines and personal trainers.

Luxury Spa

Indulge in a relaxing experience at our luxury spa, offering a variety of treatments and massages to rejuvenate your body and mind.

Restaurant

Meals at our gourmet restaurant, featuring local and international cuisines prepared by world-class chefs.
